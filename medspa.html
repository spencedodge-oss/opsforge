SOP 1 — Consultation & Intake (Front-of-House)

Owner: Provider / Front Desk · Trigger: Every new-client appointment

Purpose: Every client gets the same welcome, intake, and consultation experience — so trust and the treatment plan don't depend on which team member greets them. (Business flow; clinical decisions stay with your licensed provider.)

  • Confirm the appointment and complete intake paperwork before the client sits down.
  • Greet by name, review goals, and set clear expectations on the visit, timing, and pricing.
  • Hand off to the provider with a clean summary of goals and history; clinical assessment stays with them.
  • At checkout, review the recommended plan and rebook the next visit before the client leaves.
  • Schedule the follow-up touchpoint and log notes so the next visit starts ahead.
Quality standard: Every new client leaves with a clear next step and a booked follow-up (or a logged reason why not) — tracked, not left to chance. Clinical standards are set by your medical director, not this manual.
