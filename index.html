SOP 1 — Morning Dispatch & Route Assignment

Owner: Operations Lead · Trigger: Every workday, 7:15 AM · Time budget: 20 minutes

Purpose: Every crew leaves the shop by 7:45 AM knowing exactly where they're going, what "done" looks like at each stop, and who to call if something breaks — without the owner saying a word.

  • 7:15 — Open the day board. Confirm crew attendance against the schedule; activate the on-call sub if anyone is out.
  • 7:20 — Review today's jobs. Flag any first-time customer (attach their Quality Checklist and gate code).
  • 7:25 — Assign routes by zone, not by preference. Same crew, same zone, same weekday — customers learn faces.
  • 7:35 — Each crew lead repeats back: stops, special notes, target finish time.
  • 7:45 — Trucks roll. Anything unresolved goes to the Exceptions Log, not to the owner's phone.
Quality standard: Zero calls to the owner before 9 AM. If the owner's phone rings during dispatch hours two days in a row, this SOP gets revised — not ignored.
