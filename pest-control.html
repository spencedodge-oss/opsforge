SOP 1 — Service Stop: Inspect, Treat & Document

Owner: Service Tech · Trigger: Every service stop

Purpose: Every stop gets the same inspection, the same treatment standard, and the same clear record — so results and the customer's trust don't depend on which tech shows up.

  • Confirm the account and any prior notes before you knock; greet with the script.
  • Run the fixed interior/exterior inspection; photograph activity and conducive conditions.
  • Treat to the standard for the findings; note products and areas on the service record.
  • Walk the customer through what you found and did, and what they'll see over the next week.
  • Log the stop, flag any follow-up, and schedule the re-service if the standard calls for it.
Quality standard: Every stop documented, and no repeat activity that a completed treatment should have handled. A re-service inside the plan window is expected and free — and a note in the tech's file if a pattern shows.
