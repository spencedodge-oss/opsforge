SOP 1 — Job Start & Prep Standard

Owner: Crew Lead · Trigger: Every job, before the first coat

Purpose: Every job is protected, prepped, and set up the same way — so the finish and the client's trust don't depend on which crew shows up.

  • Walk the space with the client; confirm scope, colors, and any concern in writing before anything moves.
  • Protect and mask — floors, fixtures, edges — from the prep checklist, photographed before the first coat.
  • Surface prep to the standard: patch, sand, prime; no painting over a surface that isn't ready.
  • Paint in the fixed order; keep a wet edge; second coat only after the first passes the light check.
  • Run the final walkthrough with the client, fix anything on the spot, and confirm sign-off.
Quality standard: No callbacks on a completed job, and no client sign-off without the walkthrough. A touch-up request in the first 30 days is a no-charge return and a note in the crew's file.
